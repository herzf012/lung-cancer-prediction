Support Vector Machine

While using SVM we found that it is hard to use for this particular case study as it only allows for comparing and studying of two categories as seen in the graph. Accuracy for SVM is dependent on the sample size given and would not be able to account for all other categories. An example would be checking Dry Cough and Fatigue, while there were a lot of patients in this comparision that were Low or Medium risk of Lung Cancer it does not take into account for other conditions; such as a patient being a big drinker or the living conditions they were in having a high amount of pollution.
